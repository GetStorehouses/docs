## Overview

Your Storehouses account settings allow you to manage your profile information, email preferences, privacy settings, and data. Access your account settings at [storehouses.app/account](https://storehouses.app/account).

## Accessing Account Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Click on your profile icon or menu in the top navigation bar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Account Settings">
    Choose "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Information

### Email Address

Your email address is your primary identifier for your Storehouses account.

<ParamField path="Email" type="string" required>
  The email address associated with your account
</ParamField>

<Warning>
  Changing your email address will require verification of the new email before the change takes effect.
</Warning>

**To update your email:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account Settings">
    Navigate to [storehouses.app/account](https://storehouses.app/account)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Email Field">
    Enter your new email address in the email field
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify New Email">
    Check your new email inbox for a verification link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Change">
    Click the verification link to complete the email change
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Display Name

Set a display name for your account (optional). This name may appear in:
- Shared portfolio views
- Heir access notifications
- Export reports

### Profile Avatar

Upload a profile picture or avatar to personalize your account:
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
- Recommended size: 256x256 pixels
- Maximum file size: 2MB

## Password Management

### Changing Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Account Settings">
    Go to your account settings page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Current Password">
    Provide your current password for verification
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Create a new password that meets security requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm New Password">
    Re-enter your new password to confirm
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click "Update Password" to save
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Password Requirements

<Card title="Security Requirements" icon="lock">
  Your password must meet these criteria:
  - Minimum 8 characters
  - At least one uppercase letter
  - At least one lowercase letter
  - At least one number
  - Special characters recommended
</Card>

<Tip>
  Use a password manager to generate and store strong, unique passwords for better security.
</Tip>

### Forgot Password

If you've forgotten your password:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Login Page">
    Navigate to [storehouses.app/login](https://storehouses.app/login)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Forgot Password">
    Select "Forgot Password" link below the login form
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email">
    Provide the email address associated with your account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    Look for a password reset email from Storehouses
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Password">
    Click the reset link and create a new password
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Password reset links expire after 1 hour for security. Request a new link if yours has expired.
</Note>

## Account Actions

### Account Deactivation

Temporarily deactivate your account while preserving your data:

<Warning>
  Deactivating your account will:
  - Log you out of all devices
  - Make your portfolio inaccessible
  - Preserve all your data
  - Maintain your subscription (you'll still be billed)
  - Can be reversed at any time
</Warning>

**To deactivate your account:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account Settings">
    Navigate to your account settings page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to Danger Zone">
    Find the "Deactivate Account" section at the bottom
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Deactivate">
    Select "Deactivate Account" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm your decision in the popup dialog
  </Step>
</Steps>

**To reactivate:**
Simply log in again with your email and password. All your data will be restored immediately.

### Account Deletion

Permanently delete your account and all associated data:

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. This will:
  - Permanently delete all your items and portfolio data
  - Remove all collections and locations
  - Delete all images and documents
  - Cancel any active subscriptions
  - Cannot be undone
</Warning>

**Before deleting your account:**

<Accordion title="Export your data" icon="download">
  Download a complete export of your portfolio before deletion. This is your last chance to preserve your data.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cancel subscriptions" icon="credit-card">
  Ensure you've canceled any active subscriptions to avoid future charges. Account deletion will cancel subscriptions, but refunds are not provided.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Inform your heirs" icon="users">
  If you've set up heir access, notify your designated contacts that access will be revoked.
</Accordion>

**To delete your account:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account Settings">
    Navigate to [storehouses.app/account](https://storehouses.app/account)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Delete Account">
    Scroll to the bottom "Danger Zone" section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Delete Account">
    Select "Delete Account" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Email">
    Check your email for a deletion confirmation link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Final Confirmation">
    Click the link and provide final confirmation
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Account deletion requires email confirmation to prevent accidental deletion. The confirmation link expires after 24 hours.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't access account settings" icon="circle-exclamation">
    If you're unable to access your account settings:
    - Ensure you're logged in
    - Try refreshing the page
    - Clear your browser cache
    - Try a different browser
    - Contact support if the issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email verification not received" icon="envelope">
    If you don't receive a verification email:
    - Check your spam/junk folder
    - Verify the email address is correct
    - Wait a few minutes and check again
    - Request a new verification email
    - Contact support if needed
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Password reset not working" icon="key">
    If password reset isn't working:
    - Check that the reset link hasn't expired (1 hour limit)
    - Ensure you're clicking the most recent reset link
    - Try requesting a new reset link
    - Contact support for assistance
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
