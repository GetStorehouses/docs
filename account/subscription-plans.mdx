### Limitations

The Free plan has the following restrictions:

<Warning>
  - Maximum 50 items (portfolio limit)
  - Single collection (default "My Collection")

  - Single storage location (home)
  - No price alerts
  - No heir access functionality

  - Community support only
</Warning>

### Price Alerts (Pro & Lifetime)

Set custom alerts for asset prices:

- **Metal Spot Alerts** - Notify when gold, silver, etc. reach target prices
- **Portfolio Value Alerts** - Alert when total value hits milestones
- **Individual Item Alerts** - Track specific asset valuations
- **Delivery Options** - Email, push notifications, or both

[Learn more about Price Alerts →](/advanced/price-alerts)

### Heir Access (Pro & Lifetime)

Emergency access system for designated heirs:

- **Dual Pro Requirement**: Both account holder AND heir must have Pro/Lifetime subscriptions
- **Heir Must Have Account**: Heirs must have existing Storehouses accounts
- **Two Access Paths**:
  - Heir-initiated requests with configurable waiting periods (14-90 days)
  - Account holder pre-approval for immediate access
- **Custom Permissions**: Choose read-only or edit access per heir
- **180-Day Access**: Automatic expiration with renewal options
- **Unlimited Heirs**: Designate as many heirs as needed
- **Email Notifications**: Multiple alerts during waiting periods with one-click denial
- **Access Logging**: Audit trail of all heir access sessions

<Warning>
  Heir Access requires both the account holder AND the designated heir to maintain active Pro or Lifetime subscriptions.
</Warning>

[Learn more about Heir Access →](/advanced/heir-access)

### Export & Reporting (Pro & Lifetime)

Professional portfolio documentation:

- **PDF Reports** - Formatted documents with images and charts
- **CSV Data** - Spreadsheet-compatible data exports
- **Insurance Documentation** - Reports suitable for insurance claims
- **Tax Preparation** - Valuation summaries for tax purposes

[Learn more about Export & Reporting →](/portfolio/export-reporting)

### Share Links (Pro & Lifetime)

Secure, customizable share links for portfolio sharing:

- **Required Name**: Each link has a descriptive name for easy identification
- **QR Codes**: Auto-generated QR codes for each link (generated locally for security)
- **Password Protection**: Optional password security with bcrypt encryption
- **Expiration Dates**: Set automatic expiration for temporary access
- **View Limits**: Limit how many times a link can be accessed
- **Edit Permissions**: Choose between read-only and editable access
- **Search & Filter**: Quickly find links by name or URL
- **Inline Editing**: Modify link settings after creation
- **Copy to Clipboard**: One-click copying with visual confirmation
- **Status Badges**: Visual indicators for active, inactive, and editable links

**No Account Required**: Recipients don't need Storehouses accounts to view shared portfolios

[Learn more about Share Links →](/advanced/sharing)

## Upgrading Your Plan

### From Free to Pro

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to [storehouses.app/billing](https://storehouses.app/billing)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Pro Plan">
    Click "Upgrade to Pro" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Payment Info">
    Provide credit card details via Stripe
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Upgrade">
    Review and confirm your subscription
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instant Access">
    Pro features are immediately available
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your first Pro payment is charged immediately. Subsequent payments occur monthly on the same day.
</Note>

### From Free to Lifetime

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Pricing">
    Visit [storehouses.app/pricing](https://storehouses.app/pricing)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Lifetime">
    Click "Get Lifetime Access" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Pay the one-time $349 fee via Stripe
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lifetime Access Activated">
    All Pro features are immediately available, forever
  </Step>
</Steps>

### From Pro to Lifetime

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to your billing settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Upgrade to Lifetime">
    Choose the lifetime upgrade option
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Pay the one-time fee (Pro subscription will be canceled)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Your monthly subscription is canceled, lifetime access granted
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you upgrade from Pro to Lifetime mid-cycle, your Pro subscription is canceled immediately and you won't be charged for the next month.
</Tip>

## Downgrading Your Plan

### From Pro to Free

<Warning>
  Downgrading from Pro to Free will:
  - Disable advanced features immediately
  - Require you to reduce items to 15 or fewer
  - Preserve all your data (items remain but are inaccessible if over limit)
  - Stop future billing
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to [storehouses.app/billing](https://storehouses.app/billing)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel Subscription">
    Click "Cancel Subscription" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Downgrade Reason">
    Optionally provide feedback
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Confirm you want to downgrade to Free
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Until Period End">
    Pro features remain active until the end of your billing period
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What Happens When You Downgrade

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Items Over 50" icon="boxes">
    If you have more than 50 items:
    - All items are preserved in the database
    - You can view items but cannot add new ones
    - You must delete items to get under the 50-item limit
    - Re-upgrading to Pro instantly restores full access
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Advanced Features" icon="sparkles">
    Pro features become unavailable:
    - Live spot prices stop updating (last prices shown)
    - Price alerts are paused
    - Heir access links are deactivated
    - Export features are disabled
    - Additional storage locations remain but cannot be edited
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Data Preservation" icon="database">
    Your data is never deleted:
    - All items, collections, and locations are preserved
    - Upgrade at any time to restore full access
    - Export data before downgrading if needed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pro-Rated Billing

### Mid-Cycle Upgrades

When you upgrade to Pro mid-cycle:
- You're charged the full monthly Pro fee immediately
- Your next billing date is set to one month from the upgrade date
- No partial month charges or credits

### Refunds on Downgrades

<Warning>
  Downgrades from Pro to Free:
  - Do not receive partial refunds
  - Maintain Pro access until the end of the current billing period
  - Stop all future billing
</Warning>

### Lifetime Upgrades

When upgrading to Lifetime:
- Pro subscriptions are canceled immediately
- No partial month refunds for unused Pro time
- Lifetime access begins immediately

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I pause my Pro subscription?" icon="pause">
    Subscriptions cannot be paused, but you can cancel and re-subscribe at any time. When you cancel, you retain Pro access until the end of your billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there an annual Pro plan?" icon="calendar">
    Yes! Pro is available as both monthly ($12/month) and annual ($99/year) subscriptions. The annual plan saves you $45 per year compared to monthly billing - that's over 30% savings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I switch between monthly and annual Pro billing?" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Yes! You can switch your billing cycle at any time. [Contact support](https://storehouses.app/dashboard?support=true) to switch from monthly to annual (or vice versa). When switching, your current plan will end and the new billing cycle will begin immediately.

  <Accordion title="What happens if the Lifetime plan sells out?" icon="hourglass">
    Once 2,000 lifetime memberships are sold, the Lifetime tier will no longer be available. Existing lifetime members retain their access forever.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I transfer my Lifetime membership?" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left">
    Lifetime memberships are non-transferable and tied to your account email address.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?" icon="money-bill-transfer">

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade from Pro to Lifetime later?" icon="arrow-up">
    Yes! You can upgrade to Lifetime at any time while it's still available. Your Pro subscription will be canceled and you'll receive lifetime access.
